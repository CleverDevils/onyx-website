## The Brief

Velocity Motors needed a launch event that matched the innovation of their new electric vehicle. The reveal had to create an unforgettable moment for in-person VIPs while translating seamlessly to a global streaming audience.

## Production Elements

### The Reveal Moment
- Custom-built reveal platform with integrated lighting
- 270-degree projection mapping on vehicle and surrounding surfaces
- Synchronized pyrotechnics and atmospheric effects
- Precision timing coordinated to millisecond accuracy

### Audio Design
- Immersive 360-degree speaker array
- Custom sound design and original score
- Speech reinforcement with invisible microphones
- Simultaneous interpretation for international press

### Video Production
- 6-camera live production
- Drone footage integration
- Real-time graphics and lower thirds
- 4K HDR streaming to 12 regional feeds

## Venue Transformation

The 40,000 sq ft warehouse space was transformed into a futuristic environment featuring:
- 200' of LED ribbon displays
- Intelligent moving lights creating dynamic architecture
- Haze and atmospheric effects for beam visibility
- Climate control for guest comfort and equipment reliability

## Global Reach

The livestream reached 2.3 million viewers across 45 countries. Social media engagement exceeded projections by 300%, driven by the shareable reveal moment.
