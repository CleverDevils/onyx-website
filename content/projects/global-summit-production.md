## Event Scope

The Global Leadership Summit brings together C-suite executives from Fortune 500 companies for three days of thought leadership and networking. Our role encompassed complete AV production across the main ballroom, four breakout theaters, and multiple networking spaces.

## Production Highlights

### Main Stage
- 60' curved LED wall creating an immersive backdrop
- Confidence monitors and teleprompter systems for speakers
- Broadcast-quality lighting design
- 12-camera switching for live streaming and recording

### Breakout Sessions
- Identical AV setups across four 500-seat theaters
- Seamless speaker support with wireless presentation
- Live overflow feeds to secondary viewing areas

### Networking Technology
- Interactive digital displays throughout venue
- Mobile app integration for real-time polling
- Badge scanning and attendee tracking

## Results

The summit received its highest production ratings in the event's 15-year history. Live stream engagement increased 180% year-over-year.
