## Studio Design

### Broadcast Set
- Modular set pieces for different content formats
- 3-point interview configuration
- News desk setup for earnings calls
- Green screen capability for virtual backgrounds

### Technical Infrastructure
- 4K PTZ camera system with remote operation
- Tricaster switching and graphics platform
- Professional audio booth and voice-over room
- Direct fiber connection to major CDN providers

### Control Room
- Dedicated engineering space
- Multi-viewer monitoring wall
- Audio mixing and processing
- Recording and archival systems

## Training & Support

We provided comprehensive training for the in-house team, enabling them to produce daily content independently. Our ongoing support agreement ensures system reliability for high-stakes broadcasts.

## Impact

The studio paid for itself within 8 months through eliminated vendor costs. Meridian now produces over 200 broadcasts annually from the facility.
