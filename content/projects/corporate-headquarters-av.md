## Project Overview

This comprehensive AV installation transformed a traditional corporate headquarters into a modern, technology-driven workspace. The project encompassed multiple conference rooms, a flagship boardroom, and common areas throughout the facility.

## Scope of Work

- **15 Conference Rooms**: Each equipped with 75" displays, wireless presentation systems, and integrated video conferencing
- **Executive Boardroom**: 165" LED video wall, Dolby Atmos audio, and advanced lighting control
- **Training Center**: Divisible space with independent AV systems that can combine for large presentations
- **Digital Signage**: 25 displays throughout the facility for corporate communications

## Technology Highlights

The centerpiece of this installation is the executive boardroom featuring a custom LED video wall with 4K resolution and HDR support. The room includes:

- Beamforming microphone arrays for crystal-clear audio pickup
- AI-powered camera tracking for remote meeting participants
- Automated lighting scenes that adjust based on presentation mode
- Touch panel control at each seat position

## Results

The client reported a 40% increase in meeting efficiency and a significant improvement in remote collaboration capabilities. The system has become a showcase for visiting clients and partners.
